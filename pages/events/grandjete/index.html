Description

"Mesmera 2024 hosts an Intercollegiate Group Dance Competition, where teams compete with creative flair. Strict rules ensure fair play, including no common members between teams and a 10-minute time limit. Judges' decisions are final. Get ready to witness a showcase of talent and rhythm!"

Rules and regulations

  • Any number of teams from each college is allowed. But there should be no common members between teams.
  • Members should compulsorily bring their college ID card at the time of registration.
  • Each team must have a minimum of eight members.
  • A maximum of 25 dancing members are allowed per team. Additionally, each team can have a maximum of three non-dancing members on stage for lights, music, narration, etc.
  • Every team should have at least eight members on stage at any point of the dance.
  • Time limit: 10 minutes (inclusive of narration, setting up, etc.).
  • There are no restrictions on the choice of music. The music may contain lyrics or spoken words.
  • Rebellious behavior can result in the disqualification of a team.
  • Vulgarity in any form is strictly prohibited and may warrant disqualification.
  • The use of water, fire, or any other inflammable substance is strictly prohibited.
  • The organizers reserve the right to cancel an event or prize.
  • Props and stage settings are allowed, but no additional time for set-up will be given.
  • The rules are subject to change and will be updated on the official website.
  • The judge's decisions will be final and binding and are not open for review.
